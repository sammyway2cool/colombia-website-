Introduction

Colombia is a country in South America with diverse landscapes including mountains, rainforests, and coastlines. It is known for its rich culture, coffee, and vibrant cities.

Geography

Colombia is bordered by Venezuela, Brazil, Peru, Ecuador, and Panama, with coastlines along the Pacific Ocean and the Caribbean Sea.

Culture

Colombian culture is a mix of indigenous, African, and Spanish influences. It is famous for its music, dance (like Cumbia and Salsa), and festivals such as the Barranquilla Carnival.
